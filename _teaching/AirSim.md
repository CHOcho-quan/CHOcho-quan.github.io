A competition that using Microsoft AirSim simulation to make a UAV fly through given obstacles such as crossing ellipses & scanning aruco.

* Github Address : [AirSim Competition](https://github.com/CHOcho-quan/PX4_Tasker)
* Preliminary part of the High Score UAV Competition. We use AirSim to simulate the environment while controlling the UAV to cross those obstacles and scan the aruco. However the environment is some kind of strange since the image is only given every 0.3s. Thus the control used was combined with open-loop.
* I was the team leader of the project and in charge of the part of crossing those ellipses. It works well.
