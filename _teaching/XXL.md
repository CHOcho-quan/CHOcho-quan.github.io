Junior Year project on image processing course. We control a simple mechanical arm with two degree of freedom to play the well-known game: Kai Xin Xiao Xiao Le.

* Github Address : [Mobile Game by Robot](https://github.com/CHOcho-quan/KaiXinXiaoXiaoLe)
* Junior year second semester's project. It controls a robot to play the complex game Kai Xin Xiao Xiao Le. There are all kinds of animals there and the game is to switch those animals to finally get score from the elimination of three same animals.
* I was the team leader of the project. The robot plays it well and the video can also be got in our github.
